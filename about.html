About Avdhoot Anand

Avdhoot Anand is a modern mystic, a spiritual guide who illuminates the ancient wisdom of Tantra for the contemporary world. His teachings are rooted in the profound lineage of Dattatreya, offering a path to self-realization that is both authentic and accessible.

Having spent decades in deep sadhana and study, Avdhoot Anand translates the esoteric secrets of Tantra into practical wisdom that you can apply to your daily life. His approach is not one of escapism, but of deep engagement with the world, finding the divine in every moment and every experience.

Through his courses, consultations, and writings, he provides a roadmap to awaken your inner cosmos, to discover the limitless power and bliss that resides within you. Join him on this sacred path to transformation.
